01.

My Life And My Job

Hi, I’m Rose! Around two years ago I started learning to code. I have studied online from various up-to-date learning resources such as w3shools, MDN, Css-tricks, Stackoverflow, Youtube, and some premium Chinese websites. I subscribed to the online school Treehouse for further knowledge and practice.

I am so lucky that with my partner’s help I have had the chance to work for real customers on a couple of contracts. As a senior developer, he helped to gather requirements and assisted with some more difficult technical problems. You can see more about these completed projects here.

I think that to be a web developer, the most import thing is to have a strong self-learning ability & team cooperation spirit.

02.

How I choose to learn code

Before learning programming most of my jobs were as a sales person, both in China and in New Zealand. Some people have asked me why I chose to become a web developer when they hear I am studying seriously.

The answer is not so simple. Actually, I like any beautiful thing: a modern web design, a well decorated house and garden, or a gorgeous painting. In fact, each time when I see some nice and cool websites I feel so excited and often inspired. I plan how I can make websites like that. I enjoy writing the code behind the scenes, and seeing the end result in the browser. It is so cool, man! That is why I choose to be a web developer.

03.

My personality and hobby

I am between extroverted and Introverted. With a quiet environment I can focus on doing something for a whole day or even a whole week. But I also like to go out and make friends or meet new people. One of my favourite things to do is drink a cup of tea or coffee in the beautiful sunshine with friends or alone, for me both options are enjoyable. I also like to cook healthy and delicious food nearly every day. When I feel stressed I like to go for a walk, do yoga, or listen to some relaxing music. I try to see the positive in all things. Life is beautiful but too short, so we should cherish every day!
